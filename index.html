Why it works

Reminders you set are easy to dismiss. This one shows up and won't leave.

Four small design decisions turn a to-do list into something you actually act on.

Always on top — over everything

The window floats above every app, including other apps in full-screen, and follows you across Spaces. It's the one thing on your screen you can't scroll past.

Opens on schedule

Every hour by default — add your own custom times and toggle any on or off.

9:0010:0013:0015:3021:00

It speaks up

When it appears on schedule, a voice tells you exactly how many tasks are still pending.

Must-do, every day

Locked daily tasks reset each morning. Closing the window only snoozes them — they come right back until done.

Native & featherweight

Pure SwiftUI & AppKit, around 450 KB. No Electron, no background bloat, no sign-in — your tasks live in a plain JSON file on your Mac.

Open source

Every line is on GitHub. Read it, tweak the schedule, restyle it, and rebuild in a single command.

Put it on top. Get it done.

Download the prebuilt app, or clone and build it yourself in one command.
