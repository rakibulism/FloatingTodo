Why it works

Reminders are easy to dismiss. This one shows up and won't leave.

A few deliberate design choices turn a to-do list into something you actually act on.

Always on top — over everything

The window floats above every app, including other apps in full-screen, and follows you across Spaces. The one thing on your screen you can't scroll past. (Native macOS & Windows.)

Opens on schedule

Hourly by default — add custom times, toggle any on or off.

9:0010:0013:0021:00

It speaks up

A voice tells you exactly how many tasks are still pending when it appears.

Must-do, every day

Locked daily tasks reset each morning. Closing only snoozes them until they're done.

Native & featherweight

Around 450 KB on macOS. No Electron, no background bloat, no sign-in — your tasks live in a plain file on your device.

Open source

Every line is on GitHub. Read it, tweak the schedule, restyle it, and rebuild in one command.
